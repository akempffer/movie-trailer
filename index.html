Cinderella

Rated: PG

Classification: Drama/Fantasy

Synopsis:

Brought to life by the magic of Disney, and the lovely Lily James (Cinderella), this re-telling of a classic fairytale dazzles audiences and inspires with Ella's classic quote: "have courage and be kind". When Ella has to leave ball early, and has to run away from her Prince Charming "Kit", he is left to find a maid whom her lost glass slipper fits. With a hint of magic, and a lot of courage, this movie is the perfect fit for all ages!
